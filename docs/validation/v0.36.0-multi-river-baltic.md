# v0.36.0 Multi-river Baltic fixture specification

**Date**: 2026-04-21
**Scope**: Arc M of the WGBAST-driven improvements roadmap
**Plan**: `docs/superpowers/plans/2026-04-20-arc-M-to-Q-expanded.md`

## Purpose

Adds four WGBAST-assessment-ready Baltic river fixtures so SalmoPy can
produce per-river smolt-production output directly comparable to ICES
WGBAST Section 3 tables across the latitudinal smolt-age gradient.

## Rivers shipped

| River | AU | Latitude | Mean flow | Temp offset | smolt_min_length | PSPC total |
|-------|----|----|--------|----|-----|------|
| **Tornionjoki** | 1 | 65.85°N | 400 m³/s | −6.0°C | 14.0 cm | 2,200,000 |
| **Simojoki** | 1 | 65.60°N | 45 m³/s | −5.5°C | 14.0 cm | 95,000 |
| **Byskeälven** | 2 | 64.98°N | 40 m³/s | −3.5°C | 13.0 cm | 180,000 |
| **Mörrumsån** | S | 56.17°N | 25 m³/s | +3.0°C | 11.0 cm | 60,000 |

Temperature offsets and flow multipliers are applied relative to the
example_baltic Nemunas-basin template (mean discharge ~500 m³/s, mean
annual air temperature ~8°C at 55.7°N). Resulting per-reach
TimeSeriesInputs.csv files reflect latitude-appropriate thermal regimes.

PSPC totals match WGBAST 2026 §3 published figures within
order-of-magnitude precision (Torne 2.2M, Simo 95k, Byske 180k; Mörrum
raised to 60k per Poćwierz-Kotus et al. 2015).

## Pragmatic scaffolding note

v0.36.0 ships all 4 fixtures as **temperature/flow-modified variants of
the Nemunas-basin shapefile** rather than rewriting DBF attributes with
Torne/Simo reach names. Each reach in every fixture carries:

- `river_name: "Tornionjoki"` (etc) — used by Arc L's M74 YSFM lookup
- `pspc_smolts_per_year: <value>` — used by Arc K's PSPC output
- Per-reach `TimeSeriesInputs.csv` with latitude-appropriate temp + flow

This means **reach NAMES in the YAML stay `Nemunas`, `Atmata`, `Minija`,
…** but the biology is WGBAST-river-calibrated. A future refinement
(Arc M.4+ or Arc 0) can rewrite the shapefile DBFs to use real
`TorneUpper`, `TorneMiddle`, … reach names for downstream analytics.

## Smoke test

`tests/test_multi_river_baltic.py::test_fixture_loads_and_runs_3_days`
confirms all 4 fixtures load, run 3 days, and emit
`outmigrants.csv` + `smolt_production_by_reach_{year}.csv` with the
configured `pspc_smolts_per_year` values.

## Deferred: latitudinal-gradient smolt-age test

`test_latitudinal_smolt_age_gradient` is marked `@pytest.mark.slow` and
currently skip-gated on producing ≥5 smolts. Full 5-year runs to verify
modal smolt ages 4/3/2/2 for Torne/Simo/Byske/Mörrum require either:
(a) per-river InitialPopulations CSVs tuned to the river's density, or
(b) Arc M.4+ refinement of the fixture biology. Run manually with:

```bash
micromamba run -n shiny python -m pytest tests/test_multi_river_baltic.py \
    -v -m slow
```
